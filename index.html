Welcome to Digital Marketing Basics

When some people hear the term, "Digital Marketing," there's still a bit of confusion on what that actually is. Businesses know they need a stronger online presence, but they don't know how to go about building it. Job recruiters even find it difficult to match talent to digital marketing jobs because a lot of them have no idea what is actually needed to fill these roles. Let's face it - businesses and influencers without digital marketing efforts in place will ultimately be taken out by those that do. Whether you're a business learning about building a strong online presence, or you're an upcoming social media influencer, understanding what exactly digital marketing is, and the elements it composes of, will be your first start to staying competitive as a business or a brand.


The Digital Marketing Impact

Digital marketing has its impact-and it can be good or bad. A number of businesses are reallocating money from traditional marketing efforts to digital marketing, as businesses across the board are paying attention to the number of brick and mortar stores closing. A number of these closings are related to the increase of e-commerce, making it easier for increased competition, including from those with small budgets, but know how to make a profit selling items online. While more business is conducted online, so has marketing and advertising efforts, saving companies - both big and small - a lot of money on expensive traditional marketing and advertising efforts - and it's paying off big time!
